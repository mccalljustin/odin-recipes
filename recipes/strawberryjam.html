Strawberry Jam

Description

Peanut butter sandwich sauce. Like f'real, you aren't going to find a better supplement to that creamy peanut butter sloshed on that strong Trader Joe's wheat slice.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 4 cups white sugar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
Steps
  1. Strangle your strawberries in the most vicious manner. Get as much of the strawberry off your hand as possible into a bowl.
  2. Gently mix the strawberry slush with the sugar and lemon juice in a HEAVY BOTTOMED SAUCEPAN ok and stir over low heat until sugar evaporates?
  3. Increase heat until it hurts your hand and bring the mixture to a, and this is important now, FULL ROLLING BOIL. Boil, stirring endlessly, until mixture reaches dangerous temps.
  4. Transfer to hot! sterile! jars! leaving a thumbnail (width) for headspace and seal!.
  5. Because the jam will be eaten right away (due to its irresistibility) don't bother with processing (in a water bath?) and just refrigerate!
